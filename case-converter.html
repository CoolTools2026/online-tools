Online Case Converter Tool

Did you accidentally leave Caps Lock on? Or do you need to capitalize the first letter of every word for a title? Our Free Case Converter is a handy tool that allows you to change the capitalization of text instantly.

Available Modes

  • UPPERCASE: Converts all letters to capitals. Useful for emphasis.
  • lowercase: Converts all letters to small letters.
  • Title Case: Capitalizes the first letter of every word. Perfect for headlines and blog titles.
  • Sentence case: Capitalizes only the first letter of the first word. Best for standard writing.
  • iNVERSE cASE: Flips the case of every letter (e.g., "Hello" becomes "hELLO"). Fun for social media.
