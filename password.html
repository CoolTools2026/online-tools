Why use a Random Password Generator?

Using weak passwords like "123456" or "password" puts your personal data at risk. Hackers use sophisticated tools to crack simple passwords in milliseconds. Our Strong Password Generator creates high-entropy, random strings that are nearly impossible to guess.

Client-Side Security

Security is our top priority. Unlike other online tools, this generator runs 100% in your browser using JavaScript. The passwords you generate are never sent to our servers or stored anywhere. You can even use this tool while offline.

Best Practices for Passwords

  • Length Matters: We recommend a minimum of 12 characters. 16 characters is ideal for banking and email.
  • Mix it Up: Always use a combination of Uppercase, Lowercase, Numbers, and Symbols.
  • Don't Reuse: Never use the same password for multiple sites. Use a password manager to keep track.
