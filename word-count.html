Why Count Words?

  • Social Media: Twitter (280 chars), Instagram bios, and Facebook posts all have limits.

  • SEO: Blog posts usually aim for 1,000+ words to rank better on Google.

Character vs. Word Count

Word Count is the number of individual words (separated by spaces). Character Count includes all letters, numbers, and spaces. Our tool provides both metrics simultaneously.
